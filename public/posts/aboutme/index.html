About Me

Hello, my name is Wilson De Jesus Castillo and I adore creating games.

I am a technical artist with knowledge of using shader-graph for the Unity Engine. I was in charge of establishing the visual aesthetic for Fate’s Twisted Tower (PC) & Fate’s Twisted Mountain (PC). My goal is to continue learning the various pipelines and branches of VFX under the context of game creation.
